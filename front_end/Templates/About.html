A Tradition Of Excellence

Nkumba High School develops a feeling of togetherness among the students and staff, and parents. In this approach is an atmosphere of mutual care, respect and discipline. We also emphasize on extra-curricular activities and pastoral care to nurture a well rounded and enterprising student.

At Nkumba high school, we believe that the success of our school is measured by the value that we add to each individual student. We strive to instill an international mindset that promotes empathy for others, the acceptance of diversity, and a caring responsibility for the wider community in our students. Our dedicated team of staff help all students to achieve their full potential, preparing them for success in college and life thereafter. Our goal is to develop intellectual curiosity and to create life-long learners, through preparing students for continued success in the real world. We strive to demonstrate a superior ability to foster excellence in education and contribute to the improvement of pupils learning.We empower students with a holistic education that encourages critical thinking, creativity and innovation; and take great pleasure in the pursuit of knowledge and understanding; but most importantly, encourage our students to use their skills and intelligence to benefit the society. Every child matters at Nkumba high, and we aim to achieve the highest standards of education within a caring and stimulating environment. We are committed to ensuring that all our students achieve their full potential and enjoy their learning.

OUR HISTORY

Nkumba High School is private school established by the community to provide education to the students within and outside the community. The school started in 2010 on the request of the community. Its located in Nkumba sub-county Entebbe district about 4km from Entebbe high way.
